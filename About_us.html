About us

Our beer bar is the largest assortment of the right beer, 24 grades of intoxicating drink. And the correctness is observed from the foam manufacturer before serving the glass to the guest. In our pub all world norms of storage, pouring and giving of beer are strictly observed.



And now we have 5 restaurants in Kiev. And this is just the beginning. More than 100 beers from around the world, kilometers of pipelines with a unique system of storage, cooling and serving. The kitchen menu is developed in collaboration with the world's chefs. Tastings and Beer Education with foreign and Ukrainian craft breweries. Own service school for staff training. Performances of music groups almost every day. The most wonderful guests who believed in the dream of beer rock 'n' roll players and joined the riot. And we have big plans, goals and dreams. We are very ambitious. We are fans of quality. We are constantly learning and are not afraid to make mistakes. We love Rock and Roll.


The correct technology of bottling and serving beer, allows each guest to find their true taste: "stout", "camp", "unfiltered", "dark". In our pub a wide range of snacks to beer and very tasty food. In our menu you will find both hearty European cuisine and cooked on the grill. Comfortable rest is provided with the soulful atmosphere, characteristic and unique from all approach in service. Our beer bar is the best place to watch sports events and cultural recreation! Come, try, enjoy! We are always sincerely glad for you!
